"More than Survivors" Sermon Series
Today, Pastor Lanny continues the 8 week sermon series on what it takes 
to thrive spiritually.  Don't miss any Sunday as each week will contain 
important teaching on thriving as a follower of Christ.  If you would like 
to learn more, do the Scripture readings each day listed in the bulletin!
