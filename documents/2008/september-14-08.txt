MORE THAN SURVIVORS READING:
Leader:  Every day we are flooded with opportunities to choose.
People: Lord, help us make wise choices.
Leader: Let's reflect together on the number of choices we face every day.
(moment of silence)
Leader:  By faith, Moses refused to be known as the son of Pharaoh's daughter.
People: Lord, we choose to live as your children.
Leader: By faith, Moses chose to be mistreated along with the people of God.
People: Lord, we choose to suffer for the cause of Christ.
Leader: By faith, Moses regarded disgrace for the sake of Christ as of greater value 
than the treasures of Egypt. 
People:  Lord, we choose to store up treasures in heaven and not on earth.  Our 
citizenship is in heaven and we eagerly await a Savior from there, the Lord Jesus 
Christ!

"More than Survivors" Sermon Series
Today, Pastor Lanny starts an 8 week sermon series on what it takes to 
thrive spiritually.  Don't miss any Sunday as each week will contain 
important teaching on thriving as a follower of Christ.  If you would like 
to learn more, do the Scripture readings each day listed in the bulletin!

"More than Survivors"  Scripture readings for this week:  The focus this week 
is on making soul-conscious choices.
Sunday:  Hebrews 11:24-28
Monday:  I Peter 2:9-12
Tuesday:  Hebrews 11:13-16
Wednesday:  Deuteronomy 30:15-20
Thursday:  Matthew 16:24-28
Friday:  Romans 12-1-2
Saturday/Sunday:  Genesis 39:1-23
