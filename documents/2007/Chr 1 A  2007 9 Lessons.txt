*CALL TO WORSHIP

L:	This Christmastide it is our duty and delight to prepare ourselves to hear again the 
message of the angels,
C:	And to go in heart and mind to Bethlehem, and see this thing which is come to 
pass, and the Babe lying in a manger.
L:	Therefore, let us hear again from Holy Scripture the tale of the loving purposes of 
God from the first days of our sin,
C:	Until the glorious redemption brought us by this holy Child; and let us make this 
house of prayer glad with our carols of praise.

*OPENING PRAYER
God of manger and star, open us to the depths and heights of your incredible love 
which comes through humble stories and glorious songs. Speak to us in word and 
music and lift us to the realms of glory. Amen.
