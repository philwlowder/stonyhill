Visitors:  We warmly welcome all visitors today to our church and community!  
Please fill out one of the visitor's cards and place in the offering plate.   You are 
welcome and there is a place for you at Stony Hill!

Annual Conference:  In the weeks leading up to Annual Conference,  all United 
Methodists across the Western NC Conference are encouraged to read the Gospel of 
Matthew. (See assigned Scriptures for Matthew for this week). Matthew is a 
foundation for implementing the conference vision: Follow Jesus, Make Disciples, 
and Transform the World. You are invited to join our bishop and many United 
Methodists in fasting on Thursdays or Fridays and praying for God's will to be done 
at the Annual Conference meeting June 10-14.

Graduate Recognition Sunday:  will be Sunday, June 7.  If you have a graduate you 
would like recognized that Sunday, please contact the church office.  Graduates are 
encouraged to wear their caps and gowns and sit together in the reserved pew up front.

Hall in honor of the graduates.


Summer Schedule for the 535:  Sundays June 7 & June 21.  {No services on June

Exciting News about Summer Camp

Mount Shepherd Retreat Center, a United Methodist camp near Asheboro, NC, is 
offering another exciting summer of camp for ages 6-13.  Overnight and day camps 
will be offered.  Visit the Web site at  www.mtshepherd.org or call Mount Shepherd at
336-629-4085. You must call to make reservations.    One adult per 7 children  
required.  Call for more information about swimming, peddle boating or canoeing.



Come downtown to the Cruise-In on Friday, June 19th for a concert by the 535 
Praise Band at the Falling Rivers Art Gallery!
